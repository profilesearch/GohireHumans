Frequently Asked Questions

How do freelance platform fees work?

Freelance platforms charge fees to one or both sides of a transaction. Client fees (buyer fees) are added on top of the project price. Freelancer fees are deducted from earnings before payout. Some platforms like Toptal bundle everything into an inflated hourly rate. GoHireHumans keeps it simple: a flat 1% employer fee and 0% freelancer fee, so freelancers keep every dollar they earn.

Which platform has the lowest fees?

GoHireHumans has the lowest fees of any major freelance marketplace at just 1% paid by the employer. Freelancers keep 100% of their earnings. Upwork charges 10% to clients plus a sliding scale to freelancers (20% on the first $500, 10% up to $10K, 5% above), Fiverr charges 20% to freelancers plus 5.5% to buyers, and Toptal marks up rates by approximately 40%.

Are there hidden fees on GoHireHumans?

No. GoHireHumans charges a single, transparent 1% fee to the employer on each completed task. There are no freelancer fees, no connection fees, no subscription tiers, and no hidden charges. Escrow protection, identity verification, and full API access are all included at no extra cost.
