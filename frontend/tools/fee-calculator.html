Frequently Asked Questions

Which freelance platform has the lowest fees?

GoHireHumans has the lowest fees at just 1% paid by the employer. Freelancers keep 100% of their earnings. By comparison, Upwork charges 10-20% to freelancers, Fiverr charges 20%, and Toptal marks up rates by 40-50%.

How much does Upwork charge freelancers?

Upwork charges freelancers a sliding fee: 20% on the first $500, 10% on earnings from $500 to $10,000, and 5% above $10,000 per client. Clients also pay a 5% marketplace fee on most contracts.

How much does Fiverr take from sellers?

Fiverr takes a 20% commission from sellers on every order. Buyers also pay a 5.5% service fee. On a $100 gig, the seller gets $80 and the buyer pays $105.50.

How much can I save with GoHireHumans?

On a $5,000 project, you'd save $350-$750 compared to Upwork or Fiverr, and up to $2,500 compared to Toptal. GoHireHumans charges only 1% to employers, and freelancers keep 100%.
