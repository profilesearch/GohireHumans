What a strong post includes

Inputs

URLs, files, public sources, screenshots, AI outputs, logs, or exact prompts to review. Do not share passwords or production credentials.

Deliverable

Issue table, spreadsheet, screenshots, call log, source list, corrected text, or summary memo.

Acceptance criteria

Checkable statements that define done: number of items reviewed, proof required, deadline, and what is out of scope.
