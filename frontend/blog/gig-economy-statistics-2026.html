Market Size & Growth

  1. The global gig economy is valued between $455 billion and $646 billion (World Bank, Business Research Insights)
  2. Annual growth rates are between 16% and 17%, outpacing most traditional industries (Fortunly)
  3. The platform-driven gig economy engages approximately 12% of the global labor force (World Bank)
  4. Total freelance income in the US generates nearly $1.3 trillion annually (Fortunly)
  5. The AI agent workforce market alone is worth $7.84 billion and growing at 46% CAGR (Grand View Research)
  6. By 2030, the global freelance platform market is projected to reach $14 billion (Statista)

Earnings & Compensation

  1. The average hourly rate for freelancers in North America is $47.71 (Fortunly)
  2. Average annual income: full-time employees earn $80,116 vs. freelancers at $69,000 (Fortunly)
  3. The gap is partly explained by freelancers working 25 hours/week on average vs. 40 for full-time (Fortunly)
  4. 57% of gig workers work more than 40 hours per week; average is 43 hours (Freelancermap)
  5. The highest-paying gig job in the US is massage therapy at $27.34/hr (OysterLink)
