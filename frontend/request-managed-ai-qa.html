1. Send intake

Email the AI output, source/context, risk level, deadline, budget range, and desired deliverable.

2. Human scoping

GoHireHumans turns the request into a bounded review brief and identifies the right reviewer profile.

3. Written approval

You approve the quote and review plan before any reviewer starts or any payment-processing step happens.

4. Evidence report

You receive pass/revise/high-risk status, issue severity, evidence links/screenshots, corrections, and confidence notes.

What to include in your email

  • Review type: citation, support reply, RAG, website QA, agent audit, product/content QA, or other.
  • AI output, page URL, file, transcript, or agent log to review.
  • Source documents, policy, retrieved context, expected behavior, or acceptance criteria.
  • Deadline, rough budget, and whether this is a one-off or recurring workflow.
  • Required deliverable format: issue log, screenshots, corrected text, spreadsheet, or summary memo.
