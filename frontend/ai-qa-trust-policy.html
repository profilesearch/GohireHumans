Trust policy

AI QA scope and trust policy

Human QA can reduce risk, but it should not be misrepresented as a legal, medical, financial, or compliance guarantee.

What reviewers should provide

  • Specific issue notes
  • Source links or screenshots where relevant
  • Human-written reasoning
  • Clear pass/revise/high-risk recommendation

What buyers should provide

  • The AI output
  • Original source/context if available
  • Desired review type
  • Deadline and output format

Not a professional substitute

Human QA can catch errors and improve quality, but does not replace professional legal, medical, financial, compliance, or safety advice.

AI-use rule for reviewers

Reviewers may use tools to assist, but the final review must include human judgment, evidence, and original rationale. AI-only copy-paste reviews are not acceptable.

Best buyer habit

Before publishing or shipping AI output, get a human QA report with evidence.
