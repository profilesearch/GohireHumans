GoHireHumans use case

Local Verification Tasks

Hire nearby humans to verify places, signs, photos, listings, or real-world conditions.

Post this kind of task

Good tasks to post

  • Photo verification
  • Address or storefront checks
  • Local price/menu checks
  • Event or location confirmation

Why humans help

Local verification is useful when online data is stale, incomplete, or impossible for AI to confirm.

How to scope it

Give workers one clear goal, source material or links, a required deliverable, a deadline, and examples of what counts as acceptable work. Keep the first task small so approval is straightforward.
