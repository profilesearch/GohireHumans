Reviewer quality

AI QA reviewer badges

GoHireHumans should qualify reviewers by task type, not treat every worker as interchangeable. These badges define the first reviewer-quality system.

Fact-check reviewer

Checks claims against sources and flags hallucinations, weak evidence, and misleading statements.

Citation verifier

Checks whether citations exist, are relevant, and actually support the claim.

Support reply QA

Reviews customer-facing AI replies for policy fit, tone, correctness, and escalation risk.

RAG groundedness reviewer

Compares AI answers with retrieved docs and identifies unsupported statements.

Product listing QA

Checks ecommerce listings for accuracy, completeness, risky claims, and clarity.

Agent audit reviewer

Reviews AI agent output and available trace/context for action appropriateness.

Calibration standards

  • Reviewer completes sample tasks before receiving badge.
  • Reports must include human-written rationale and evidence.
  • Repeated low-effort or AI-only reviews lose eligibility.
  • Quality should be tracked by category, not only global rating.
