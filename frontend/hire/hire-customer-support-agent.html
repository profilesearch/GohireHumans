Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a freelance customer support agent cost?

Freelance customer support agents charge $15-40/hr. Basic email support starts at $15-22/hr, while multi-channel support costs $22-32/hr. Senior leads handling escalations charge $32-40+/hr.

What tools should a support agent know?

Essential tools include Zendesk, Freshdesk, or Intercom for ticket management. CRM experience (Salesforce, HubSpot) and live chat tools are important. Phone support requires VoIP familiarity.

Can I hire support agents for weekends and holidays?

Yes. GoHireHumans has a global talent pool, making it easy to find agents available for evenings, weekends, and holidays. Specify your schedule requirements in the job posting.

How do I ensure quality customer service?

Use GoHireHumans escrow to review work before paying. Set clear KPIs (response time, resolution rate, CSAT score), provide a knowledge base, and start with a trial period.
