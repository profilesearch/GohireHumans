What Does a VA Do?

A virtual assistant (VA) is a remote professional who provides administrative, technical, or creative support to businesses and entrepreneurs from anywhere in the world. Instead of hiring a full-time employee with overhead costs like office space, benefits, and equipment, a VA delivers the same productivity at a fraction of the price. Whether you are a solo founder drowning in emails, a growing ecommerce brand struggling to keep up with customer inquiries, or an executive who needs a second brain to manage a packed schedule, a virtual assistant becomes an extension of your team.

The tasks a VA can handle span virtually every aspect of business operations. Here are the most common services you can hire a virtual assistant for on GoHireHumans:

  • Administrative support — General office tasks like filing, organizing documents, preparing presentations, and managing correspondence so you can focus on high-value work
  • Email management — Inbox triage, drafting responses, filtering spam, flagging priorities, and managing follow-ups so nothing falls through the cracks
  • Calendar and scheduling — Coordinating meetings across time zones, booking appointments, sending reminders, and resolving scheduling conflicts
  • Data entry and database management — Updating spreadsheets, CRM systems like Salesforce or HubSpot, inventory records, and maintaining accurate business data
  • Customer support — Responding to customer inquiries via email, live chat, or phone; processing returns; handling escalations; and maintaining satisfaction scores
  • Research — Market research, competitor analysis, lead generation, product sourcing, price comparisons, and compiling actionable reports
  • Social media management — Scheduling posts across platforms, engaging with followers, monitoring brand mentions, responding to comments, and creating basic content
  • Bookkeeping and finance — Invoice creation, expense tracking, receipt organization, bank reconciliation, and light financial reporting using tools like QuickBooks or Xero

Many businesses start by delegating a single task — like email management — and quickly realize the value of having a dedicated VA. From there, the relationship grows to cover scheduling, customer support, and beyond. If you also need specialized data entry support, GoHireHumans has dedicated professionals for that as well.
