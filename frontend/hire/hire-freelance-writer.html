What Does a Freelance Writer Do?

A freelance writer creates written content for businesses, publications, and individuals on a project or retainer basis. From SEO-optimized blog posts that drive organic traffic to high-converting sales copy that turns visitors into customers, freelance writers are the engine behind effective content marketing. Unlike hiring an in-house writer with salary, benefits, and overhead, a freelance writer gives you access to specialized expertise exactly when you need it — whether that is a one-time product launch or an ongoing content calendar.

The demand for quality written content has never been higher. Search engines reward websites that publish consistent, well-researched content. Email campaigns with compelling copy generate measurable ROI. And businesses that invest in thought leadership writing build trust and authority in their markets. Here are the most common types of writing you can hire a freelance writer for on GoHireHumans:

  • Blog posts and articles — Long-form and short-form content optimized for search engines, designed to attract organic traffic, educate your audience, and establish your brand as an authority in your industry
  • Website copywriting — Landing pages, about pages, product descriptions, and service pages that communicate your value proposition clearly and persuade visitors to take action
  • Technical writing — User manuals, API documentation, knowledge base articles, white papers, and standard operating procedures that make complex information accessible and usable
  • Email marketing — Newsletter content, drip campaigns, promotional sequences, onboarding emails, and re-engagement campaigns that nurture leads and drive conversions
  • SEO content — Keyword-researched, search-optimized articles and pages designed to rank on Google, drive organic traffic, and capture high-intent search queries
  • Ghostwriting — Books, thought leadership articles, LinkedIn posts, and byline pieces written on behalf of executives, entrepreneurs, and public figures
  • Social media content — Platform-specific captions, scripts, thread copy, and ad text for Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter/X, Facebook, and TikTok
  • Press releases and PR content — Newsworthy announcements, case studies, brand storytelling, and media pitches crafted to earn coverage and build credibility

Great content does not happen by accident. It requires research, structure, a clear understanding of the target audience, and the ability to write in a voice that resonates. A skilled freelance writer brings all of this to the table, allowing you to focus on running your business while your content pipeline keeps producing results.

What is the difference between a content writer and a copywriter?
Content writers create informational, educational, or entertaining content — like blog posts, articles, and guides — designed to build authority and attract organic search traffic over time. Copywriters write persuasive text — like sales pages, ads, and email campaigns — designed to drive specific actions such as purchases, sign-ups, or downloads. Content writing focuses on long-term traffic and trust; copywriting focuses on immediate conversions. Many freelance writers offer both services, but the skill sets and pricing models can differ significantly.
