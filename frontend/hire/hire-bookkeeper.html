Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a freelance bookkeeper cost?

Freelance bookkeepers charge $20-50/hr depending on experience. Basic bookkeeping starts at $20-30/hr, while CPA-level work costs $40-50+/hr. On GoHireHumans, you pay just 1% employer fee on top.

What software should a bookkeeper know?

Essential software includes QuickBooks (Online or Desktop), Xero, FreshBooks, and Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets. Experience with payroll platforms like Gusto or ADP is a plus.

Is it safe to hire an online bookkeeper?

Yes, when using platforms with identity verification and escrow protection like GoHireHumans. Always use NDAs for financial data and start with a small test project to verify quality.

How often should a bookkeeper update my books?

Most small businesses benefit from weekly or bi-weekly bookkeeping updates. Monthly is the minimum for accurate financial reporting. Year-end cleanup should happen in Q1 for tax preparation.
