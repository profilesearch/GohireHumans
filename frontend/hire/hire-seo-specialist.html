Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a freelance SEO specialist cost?

SEO specialists charge $30-100+/hr. Basic audits and keyword research start at $30-50/hr, while comprehensive strategy and link building costs $50-75/hr. Senior consultants charge $75-100+/hr. Monthly retainers typically range from $1,000-5,000.

How long does SEO take to show results?

Most SEO campaigns show initial improvements in 3-6 months, with significant results in 6-12 months. Technical fixes may show faster results. Be cautious of anyone promising guaranteed rankings or overnight results.

What should I look for when hiring an SEO specialist?

Look for proven results (case studies with before/after traffic data), knowledge of current Google algorithm updates, technical skills, transparent reporting practices, and realistic expectations. Avoid anyone guaranteeing #1 rankings.

Do I need an SEO specialist or an agency?

Freelance SEO specialists offer more personalized attention at $30-100/hr compared to agencies at $2,000-10,000+/month. For small to mid-size businesses, a skilled freelancer on GoHireHumans (with a 1% GoHireHumans fee plus Stripe processing) is typically the better value.
