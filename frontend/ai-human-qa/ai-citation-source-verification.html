AI citation QA

Verify AI citations before they become public mistakes.

Use GoHireHumans to hire a human reviewer who checks whether AI-generated links, sources, quotes, statistics, and citations are real, relevant, and actually support the claim.

Good first tasks

Bound the review to one AI draft, source list, or spreadsheet so a reviewer can return clear evidence.

broken linkssource supportquoted textstatisticscitation mismatch

Suggested payout: $15–$75 for a focused citation/source pass; more for long reports or batches.

What reviewers check

  • Each source exists and opens.
  • The cited page supports the specific claim.
  • Quotes and statistics are copied accurately.
  • Dates, names, and numbers match the source.
  • Weak, irrelevant, or missing sources are flagged.

What buyers should provide

  • The AI-generated draft or answer.
  • All source links or retrieved documents.
  • The claims that matter most.
  • Any preferred citation style or output format.
  • Deadline and budget range.

Expected deliverable

Ask for a table with claim, source URL, pass/revise/high-risk status, issue severity, notes, and corrected wording where useful.
