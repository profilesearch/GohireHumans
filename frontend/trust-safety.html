Trust & Safety

Know what to review before anyone commits

GoHireHumans is a marketplace and payment connector. Profiles may display trust signals where available, and support can review task issues using platform records and available evidence.

Profile signals where available
Stripe checkout where configured
Evidence-based issue review
Content screening and reporting

Three checks before you proceed

1 Check the provider and scope

Profiles may show identity status, experience, work samples, ratings, and reviews where available. Those signals are not a guarantee.

  • Compare the profile with the specific task and requested result.
  • Define inputs, exclusions, timing, price, and acceptance criteria.
  • Keep task communication on-platform where possible so there is a usable record.
2 Review payment and issue terms

Stripe-powered processing where configured. Workers receive the listed payout. Employers pay Stripe processing plus a 1% GoHireHumans fee where payment processing is configured.

Payment Review and dispute handling. Issue review uses configured processor rules, platform records, and available evidence; an outcome is not guaranteed.

  • Supported checkout flows use Stripe where configured; payment availability can vary by workflow.
  • Review the listed scope, provider details, and deliverables before approving paid work.
  • Revision, refund, and other payment outcomes follow configured processor rules, platform records, and available evidence.
  • Off-platform payment requests can bypass platform records and issue-review evidence.
3 Protect safety and sensitive information

Fraud, fake reviews, privacy violations, explicit content, dangerous physical tasks, and other prohibited work can trigger review or account action.

  • Do not put passwords, credentials, payment instructions, or sensitive customer data in public listings.
  • Share only the information needed for the scoped task and use account security controls.
  • GoHireHumans does not sell user data to advertisers. See the Privacy Policy for collection, use, and sharing details.
