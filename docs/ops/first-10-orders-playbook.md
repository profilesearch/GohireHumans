# GoHireHumans first-10-orders operating system

Purpose: operate the first 10-20 orders as a concierge agency wearing a marketplace shell. Keep tasks small, scoped, evidence-backed, and founder-reviewed where feasible.

## Intake checklist

1. Buyer identity and approved contact channel.
2. Starter SKU or custom task type.
3. Exact input/assets to inspect.
4. What is in scope and explicitly out of scope.
5. Deadline and expected proof artifacts.
6. Data sensitivity and credential handling.
7. Case-study/testimonial permission: yes/no/later.

## Order stages

1. Scope confirmed in writing.
2. Payment/order routed through GoHireHumans where feasible.
3. Worker/founder assigned.
4. Checklist executed.
5. Founder review before buyer delivery where feasible.
6. Proof pack delivered.
7. Buyer acceptance/revision captured.
8. Testimonial/case-study/referral ask.
9. Retro logged: margin, quality, redo risk, worker fit, checklist changes.

## Non-negotiables

- Do not promise cash-liability guarantees without approval.
- Do not accept vague/unbounded work.
- Do not expose sensitive data publicly.
- Do not route payment off-platform.
- Mark unknowns and uncertainty clearly.

## Kill/review criteria

If 60+ serious buyer outreaches and at least 5 serious conversations produce fewer than 3 paid orders, review or pivot the wedge.
